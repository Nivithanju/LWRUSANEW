5K Run4Love

Race Details

What: 5KRun4Love Charity & Competitive Run

When: September 10, 2022

  • Walk-in Registration and Packet Pick-up from 7:45a.m. - 8:45 A.M.
  • Run begins at 9 A.M.

Where: Tennessee Riverpark, Shelter #4, 4301 Amnicola Hwy, Chattanooga, 37402.

Why: September is Craniofacial Acceptance Month. Come and run in honor of all of our cleft heroes around the globe!

Are You Ready?

The 5KRun4Love is a unique opportunity to raise awareness and funds to help these craniofacial patients get free surgeries. Get involved and be a part of changing lives and changing destinies. Register today and enjoy your 5Krun4love T-Shirt, swag bag plus an amazing fun-packed morning.

Thank you for believing in the vision, Because Every Child Matters!
